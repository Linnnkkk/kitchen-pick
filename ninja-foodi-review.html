Ninja Foodi DualZone Air Fryer Review

Check Price on Amazon

Overview

The Ninja Foodi DualZone 8-quart Air Fryer is the most versatile air fryer we tested in 2026. Its standout feature is dual independent baskets — you can cook two completely different foods at different temperatures and times, and with Smart Finish, both baskets finish cooking at the same time. For households that need to juggle mains and sides, this feature alone justifies the price.

We tested the Foodi DualZone daily for three weeks, cooking everything from frozen fries and chicken wings to roasted vegetables and salmon fillets. It delivered consistent, even results across all cooking modes, with the only real trade-off being its size — at 16.5 x 14.6 x 13.3 inches, it demands serious counter space.

At $200, it sits in the premium range for air fryers. But the dual-basket design means you're effectively getting two cooking zones in one unit, which eliminates the need to cook in batches. For families of 3-5, it's worth every penny.

Performance Testing

Air Frying

We air fried frozen french fries, chicken wings, and breaded shrimp across both baskets simultaneously. Fries came out golden and crispy in 14 minutes at 390°F — no flipping required. Chicken wings were perfectly crispy-skinned and juicy inside at 400°F for 22 minutes. Both baskets produced identical results, confirming even heat distribution across zones.

Roasting

We roasted a mix of broccoli, sweet potatoes, and bell peppers at 375°F. Vegetables caramelized nicely without burning, retaining good texture. The 4-quart basket comfortably fits a full sheet's worth of vegetables for 2-3 people.

Baking

We baked chocolate chip cookies and a small batch of muffins. Cookies baked evenly in 10 minutes at 350°F with a slightly chewy center — results were comparable to a conventional oven. Muffins rose properly and browned on top. This isn't a full oven replacement, but it handles small baking tasks well.

Smart Finish (Dual-Zone Cooking)

This is the Foodi DualZone's killer feature. We tested it by cooking salmon at 400°F for 12 minutes in one basket and asparagus at 375°F for 8 minutes in the other. Smart Finish delayed the asparagus start so both finished at the same time — hot and ready to serve together. It works exactly as advertised and eliminates the "keep one warm while the other cooks" problem.

Dehydrating

We made beef jerky using the dehydrate function at 160°F. After 6 hours, the jerky was properly dried with good texture. It works, but the small capacity means you can only make a small batch at a time. Dedicated dehydrators will serve you better if that's your primary use case.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you use both baskets at different temperatures?

Yes. Each basket has independent temperature and time controls. You can air fry chicken at 400°F on one side and roast vegetables at 375°F on the other.

What is Smart Finish?

Smart Finish synchronizes two different cooking programs so both baskets finish at the same time. The air fryer automatically delays the start of the shorter program so everything is ready together.

Is it easy to clean?

Yes. Both baskets and crisper plates are ceramic-coated and dishwasher-safe. In our testing, most residue wiped off easily with warm soapy water. We recommend hand-washing to preserve the nonstick coating longer.

How does it compare to the Ninja Crispi?

The DualZone has two baskets and double the total capacity (8 qt vs 6 qt), plus the Smart Finish feature. The Crispi is more compact, $40 cheaper, and better for smaller households or limited counter space. See our best air fryers roundup for the full comparison.

What can you cook in it?

It supports 6 cooking functions: air fry, air broil, roast, bake, reheat, and dehydrate. You can cook everything from french fries and chicken wings to roasted vegetables, salmon, cookies, and beef jerky.
